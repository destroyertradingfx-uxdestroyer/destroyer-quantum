## ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS

### Problem 1: Trade Slot Contention
The dispatch chain runs strategies in order. New A-Tier strategies (TBM, PO3, FractalModel, APlusFVG, CRT) run BEFORE the proven strategies (Phantom, Reaper, Nexus). When the new strategies open trades, they consume InpMaxOpenTrades slots. By the time Phantom/Reaper/Nexus try to execute, there's no room.

V28.06 had ~6 strategies competing for slots.
V28.12 has ~17 strategies competing for the same slots.
The proven strategies got starved.

### Problem 2: Correlated Losses
When TBM, PO3, and FractalModel all lose simultaneously (which they do — they're all directional strategies), the account drawdown compounds. Three losers hitting at once = 3x the drawdown of one loser.

### Problem 3: New Strategies Don't Complement — They Conflict
TBM, PO3, and FractalModel all try to trade directional breakouts. They're not diversifying — they're triplicating the same bet. When the bet is wrong, all three lose.
