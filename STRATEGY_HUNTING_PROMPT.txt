   - Must integrate with the magic number system (unique magic number)
   - Must work with the Queen Bee money management (lot sizing is handled externally)

5. RISK-DEFINED
   - Every trade must have a stop loss (no unlimited risk)
   - SL should be ATR-based (adaptive to volatility), not fixed pips
   - Target R:R of at least 1.5:1 (preferably 2:1 or better)
   - Strategy must have a maximum concurrent position limit

   - Must define which trading sessions it operates in (London, NY, Asian, overlap)
   - Must NOT trade during high-impact news events (system handles this via Event Shield)
   - Best if it has a clear "when to trade" and "when NOT to trade" logic
