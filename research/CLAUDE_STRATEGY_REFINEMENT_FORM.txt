WHAT WE TRIED (AND WHAT WENT WRONG)
------------------------------------

1. All 5 strategies use EMA20 on H4 as their sole bias filter.
   -> This is too simplistic. On H4, EMA20 flips frequently, causing
      whipsaws. The proven strategies (Phantom, NB) use multi-factor
      confluence, not a single EMA.

2. TBM, PowerOf3, Fractal produced ZERO trades in backtesting.
   -> Their entry conditions are too narrow/restrictive for H4 data.
   -> TBM: requires sweep of previous candle + reversal close + EMA bias
      -> On 10,925 H4 bars, this combination never occurred.
   -> PO3: requires body > 50% of range + direction alignment
      -> Too restrictive as a standalone filter.
   -> Fractal: requires 3 consecutive higher lows (or lower highs)
      -> Rare on H4 without additional confirmation.

3. APlusFVG and CRT were disabled as losers (produced negative trades).
   -> APlusFVG: FVG detection + breakout + EMA20/50 alignment
      -> The FVG detection code may have bugs (checking i-1, i+1 indices
         which shift on every new bar). Entry timing is wrong.
   -> CRT: sweep of candle range + EMA alignment
      -> Entry conditions overlap with TBM (both look for sweeps).

4. Risk filters killed the EA:
   -> VAR limit (threshold 1.0) blocked nearly ALL trades in final hours.
   -> ADX=57.6 "regime suppression" blocked Reaper BUY grid.
   -> These are system-level issues, not strategy-specific, but the
      strategies' poor performance triggered aggressive risk responses.

5. All strategies use the same exit: fixed RR ratio (InpRR_Ratio, default 2.0)
   -> No trailing, no partial TP, no time-based exit.
   -> One-size-fits-all exit doesn't work across different strategy types.


THE 5 STRATEGIES — CURRENT IMPLEMENTATION
------------------------------------------

Each strategy: (a) checks for existing open trades with same magic number,
(b) determines bias, (c) checks entry conditions, (d) executes with fixed SL/TP.

--- STRATEGY 1: TBM (Time-Based Manipulation) | Magic 9010 ---

Logic: Sweep of previous candle's low/high + reversal close, filtered by EMA20 bias.

Entry (BUY):
  - Close[0] > EMA20 on H4 (bullish bias)
  - Low[0] < Low[1] (current bar swept below previous bar's low)
  - Close[0] > Open[0] (bullish close)
  - Close[0] > Low[1] (closed above the swept level)

Entry (SELL):
  - Close[0] < EMA20 on H4 (bearish bias)
  - High[0] > High[1] (current bar swept above previous bar's high)
  - Close[0] < Open[0] (bearish close)
  - Close[0] < High[1] (closed below the swept level)

SL: Low[0] - 10pts (buy) / High[0] + 10pts (sell)
TP: SL distance * RR ratio (default 2.0)

Problem: This exact combination of conditions (sweep + reversal + EMA alignment)
NEVER occurred in 10,925 H4 bars. The conditions are too specific.

--- STRATEGY 2: PowerOf3 (PO3) | Magic 9011 ---

Logic: Expansion candle (large body, small wicks) in direction of EMA20 bias.

Entry (BUY):
  - Close[0] > EMA20 on H4 (bullish bias)
  - (High[0] - Low[0]) > 0 (valid range)
  - |Close[0] - Open[0]| / (High[0] - Low[0]) >= 0.5 (body >= 50% of range)
  - Close[0] > Open[0] (bullish candle)

Entry (SELL):
  - Close[0] < EMA20 on H4 (bearish bias)
  - |Close[0] - Open[0]| / (High[0] - Low[0]) >= 0.5
  - Close[0] < Open[0] (bearish candle)

SL: Low[0] - 10pts (buy) / High[0] + 10pts (sell)
TP: SL distance * RR ratio

Problem: Body >= 50% of range is common, but combined with EMA20 alignment
the signals are noisy. No volume or context confirmation. Produces many
false signals or none depending on market conditions.

--- STRATEGY 3: Fractal Model | Magic 9012 ---

Logic: 3-candle pattern of consecutive higher lows (buy) or lower highs (sell),
confirmed by EMA20 bias and directional close.

Entry (BUY):
  - Close[0] > EMA20 on H4
  - Low[0] > Low[1] > Low[2] (3 consecutive higher lows)
  - Close[0] > Open[0] (bullish close)

Entry (SELL):
  - Close[0] < EMA20 on H4
  - High[0] < High[1] < High[2] (3 consecutive lower highs)
  - Close[0] < Open[0] (bearish close)

SL: Low[2] - 10pts (buy) / High[2] + 10pts (sell)
TP: SL distance * RR ratio

Problem: Strict 3-bar structure + EMA alignment rarely occurs on H4.
Needs relaxation or additional entry triggers (e.g., retest of the fractal level).

--- STRATEGY 4: APlusFVG (Fair Value Gap + Breakout) | Magic 9013 ---

Logic: Detect FVG at breakout of previous day's range, filtered by EMA20/50 alignment.

Entry (BUY):
  - Time < 17:00 server
  - Previous day range (High[1D] - Low[1D]) >= 20 pips
  - EMA20 > EMA50 on H4 (trend alignment)
  - FVG detected: Low[i-1] > High[i+1] for i in 1..3
  - Close[i] > previous day high (breakout)
  - Price currently inside FVG zone (Ask between fvgHigh and fvgLow)

Entry (SELL): inverse conditions.

SL: Low[3] - 10pts / High[3] + 10pts
TP: SL distance * RR ratio

Problem: FVG detection may have index bugs (shifting i-1/i+1 references).
Day-range breakout + FVG + EMA triple alignment is extremely rare.
The FVG zone check (price must be IN the gap) conflicts with the breakout
(price already moved beyond the gap).

--- STRATEGY 5: CRT (Candle Range Theory) | Magic 9014 ---

Logic: Session-based sweep of candle range, filtered by EMA20/50 alignment.

Entry (BUY):
  - Time: 07:00-20:00 server (London+NY sessions)
  - EMA20 > EMA50 on H4 (bullish alignment)
  - Close[0] > EMA20
  - Low[1] < Low[2] (previous bar swept below bar-before's low)
  - Close[1] > Open[1] (previous bar closed bullish)

Entry (SELL): inverse conditions.

SL: Low[1] - 10pts / High[1] + 10pts
TP: SL distance * RR ratio

Problem: Overlaps heavily with TBM (both look for sweeps).
EMA20/50 alignment is stricter than EMA20 alone but the sweep condition
uses previous bar data, creating a 1-bar delay that misses the move.
