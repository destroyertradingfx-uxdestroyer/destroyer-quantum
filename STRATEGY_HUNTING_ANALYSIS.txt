## REJECTED: SENTINEL TWIST (Magic 224713)

### Why It's Risky:
- Z-score mean reversion (buy when price is 2 std devs below mean) is DANGEROUS
- During strong trends, price can stay 2+ std devs from mean for WEEKS
- This is the exact trap that blew up V28.09 (adding trades against the trend)
- The "volatility compression" filter (ATR < median ATR) doesn't help —
  strong trends often have LOW volatility (steady directional move)
- RSI < 32 + z-score < -2.0 = buying into a crash, not a reversal
- The time-based exit code has a BUG: `iBars(NULL,0)-OrderOpenTime()/Period()*60`
  is mathematically wrong (mixing bar count with timestamp)

### The Math Problem:
If EURUSD drops 200 pips in a strong downtrend, z-score hits -2.5.
Strategy buys. Price drops another 100 pips. SL at 1.2 ATR (~100 pips) gets hit.
This repeats 3-4 times = -$400 per trade x 4 = -$1,600 in one trend.
One bad trend can wipe out a year of wins.

## REJECTED: QUANTUM GAP RAIDER (Magic 9004)

### Why It's Rejected:
1. **Uses MT5 syntax** — MqlTradeRequest, MqlTradeResult, OrderSend(request, result)
   are MT5 functions. This WILL NOT COMPILE in MQL4. Dead on arrival.
2. **Magic 9004 is taken** — our old DivergenceMR uses this number
3. **Gap definition is wrong** — `Low(N-1) > High(N-2) + 0.5*ATR(N-2)` is a
   150+ pip gap on EURUSD H4. These essentially never happen.
4. **The `start()` function** at the bottom is for old MQL4 builds (pre-MetaTrader 4 build 600).
   Modern MQL4 uses OnTick() only.
